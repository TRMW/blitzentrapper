"American Goldwing" Album Presale!

Finally! We're so excited to almost be able to share our new album, American Goldwing, with all of you. Below, you'll be able to pre-order the new album on limited edition white vinyl, black vinyl, CD, or digital download, and can choose to add a sweet new American Goldwing-themed shirt to your order!

Even better news — all pre-orders will receive access to an exclusive stream of the album beginning August 16th until the official release date, so it's less than two weeks until you will be album to hear American Goldwing in it's entirety!

To ensure your order will be delivered on September 13th, it will need to be placed by September 6th for the US and Canada, and September 1st for the rest of the world. Shipping charges vary by territory. All pre-order customers will receive an email with instructions on how to access the live album stream after it's activated on August 16th. All shirts are 50/50 blend unisex American Apparel tees.

Can't wait for you to hear the album, and hope to see you on tour this fall!
